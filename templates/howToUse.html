How to use Post.it

Our web application allows you to create your own "poster" - you can personalize what twitter feeds, youtube videos, and pinterest boards
you can have on your page. Even when you refresh and/or log out, your poster will remain the same.

Twitter: For Twitter, you will need to copy and paste the link of a twitter profile (i.e. https://twitter.com/humansofny) into the space provided.

Youtube: For Youtube, you will need to copy and paste a youtube video link (i.e. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=31nOaXSeqSo) into the space provided.

Pinterest: For Pinterest, you will need to copy and paste a Pinterest board or user (i.e. https://www.pinterest.com/jaimieyeah/) into the space provided.

You can also click on the "Tweet" button, which will prompt another window and allow you to post on Twitter without having to open another window yourself.
Additionally, the "Pin It" button will allow you to pin images from our website onto your Pinterest account.

Happy posting!
